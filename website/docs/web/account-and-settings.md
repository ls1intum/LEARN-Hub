# Account Management and Offline Materials

## Account Dashboard

Access your account dashboard by clicking your profile icon in the top menu.

[SCREENSHOT: Account dashboard]

### Update Profile

1. Click **Edit Profile**
2. Update your information:
   - Email address
   - First name
   - Last name
   - Password (leave blank to keep current password)
3. Click **Save Changes**

### Delete Account

To remove your account permanently:

1. Scroll to **Delete Account** section
2. Type **Delete** to confirm
3. Click **Delete**

> ⚠️ **Warning**: Deleting your account removes all saved favorites and search history. This action cannot be undone.
