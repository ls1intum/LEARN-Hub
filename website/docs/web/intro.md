# Welcome to LEARN-Hub

LEARN-Hub helps teachers discover and plan computer science activities for their classrooms. Find age-appropriate activities, generate complete lesson plans with AI-assisted recommendations, and save your favorites for future use.

LEARN-Hub runs entirely in your web browser – there is nothing to install. You can start browsing immediately as a guest, or sign in to save favorites and keep a search history.

## Key Features

- **Guided Recommendations** – A step-by-step configurator asks about your class (age, time, room, available tools, learning goals) and returns ranked activities with a transparent scoring breakdown
- **Lesson Planning** – Combine multiple activities into a timed lesson plan with automatic breaks
- **Browse & Search the Library** – Explore the complete library of curated computer science activities and filter by your exact needs
- **Save & Reuse** – Mark activities and lesson plans as favorites and re-run any previous search from your history
- **Offline Materials** – Download activity documents and lesson plans as PDFs for the classroom
