Save activities and recommendations for quick access later. Track your previous searches and re-run them anytime.

## Saving Favorites

### Add to Favorites

Click the **heart icon** on any activity or recommendation to save it. Requires login.

- **Activities** â€“ Individual activities you've bookmarked
