# Create Activity Recommendations

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl';

The **Recommendation Configurator** is the primary entry point of the LEARNApp. It allows teachers to generate activity sets based on classroom context and teaching goals.

## Overview

- Guided, multi-step process to personalize recommendations
- Parameters include: Duration, Grade, Topic, and Available Devices
- Results display the most relevant and age-appropriate activities

## Steps for Creating Activity Recommendations

1. Open the **LEARNApp Planung** tab from the navigation bar.
2. Select the duration (e.g., 30 or 45 minutes).
3. Choose the grade level (1â€“4) to ensure suitable difficulty.
4. Deselect any materials or digital devices that are not available in your classroom.
5. Deselect any topics you do not want to include.
6. Use the progress bar at the top to navigate between steps and adjust your selections at any time.
7. Tap **Continue** to generate your personalized activity recommendations.
8. Browse the list of recommendations and open a recommendation card for details.
9. Download the recommendation as a PDF for offline use.
