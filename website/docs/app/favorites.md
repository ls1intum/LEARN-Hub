# Favorites

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl';

**LEARNApp** lets you save both individual activities and complete recommendation sets for quick access later.
Saved items can be found under the **Favorites** tab in the navigation bar or in your **Profile**.

## Steps for Managing Favorites

1. **Save an activity**: Tap the empty heart icon ğŸ¤ next to an activity in the **Search** tab to add it to your favorites.
2. **Save a recommendation**: Tap the empty heart icon ğŸ¤ next to a recommendation in the **Plan** tab to save the entire activity set. When saving a recommendation, you can assign a custom name to make it easier to find later.
3. Open **Favorites** tab from the navigation bar to access your favorites. The page contains two tabs:
- **Activities**: Displays all saved individual activities.
- **Recommendations**: Displays all saved activity sets you created.
