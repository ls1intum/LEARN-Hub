# Account Management

An account is required only if you want to **save favorites** or access your **recommendation history**.  
You can still **browse**, **search**, and **generate activity recommendations** without logging in.

## Steps for Account Creation

1. Tap **Create Account** button in the Onboarding page, **Favorites** tab or **Profile** tab in the navigation bar.

## Login

You can log in using either of the following methods:

- **Email and Password:** Enter your registered email address and password.  
- **Email and Code:** Enter your email address to receive a **6-digit verification code** by email, then use it to log in temporarily without a password.  

> üîÑ *You can switch between both login methods at any time.*

## Profile Setting

You can access your profile from the navigation bar to manage your personal information and account preferences.

### Available Actions
- **Edit Profile Information:** Update your **name** and **email address**.  
- **Change Password:** Set a new password for your account.  
- **Log Out:** Safely sign out of your current session.  
- **Delete Account:** Permanently remove your account and all associated data from the system.  

> ‚ö†Ô∏è *Deleting your account cannot be undone. All saved favorites and history will be lost.*
