# History

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl';

The **History** section in your **Profile** keeps a record of all previously generated recommendations.  
It allows you to revisit, reuse, or manage your past searches at any time.

## Steps for Using History

1. Open the **LEARNApp Profile** tab in the navigation bar.
2. Tap **History** to view your complete list of past recommendations.
3. Browse the list of all previously generated recommendations.
4. Tap an entry to **reopen it** - the app automatically reloads the input parameters and displays the corresponding results.
